## Project Story

```
## The Problem: Public Money Behind Closed Doors

450 million EU citizens have government-verified digital identity (eIDAS). 
But none of them can use it to transparently govern shared funds on-chain.

Public budgets are opaque. Elections rely on blind trust. Creator platforms take 45% cuts. There is no bridge between EU legal identity and blockchain transparency.

## Our Solution: eIDAS Identity Meets Safe Treasury

We connect Croatian eIDAS identity (Certilia Mobile.id) with Safe smart accounts on Gnosis Chain. Citizens prove "I am a unique, verified person" using ZK nullifiers — without revealing WHO they are.

**How it works:**
1. Citizen authenticates via Certilia (government eIDAS app)
2. Client generates a ZK proof of identity (no personal data on-chain)
3. Smart contract verifies the proof and mints a Soulbound Token
4. Safe treasury recognizes the SBT for fund governance

Every euro is tracked on-chain. Every payout is publicly auditable. No intermediary holds the keys.

## Proof of Concept: Podcast Marketplace

We start with a podcast marketplace where creators receive EURe (regulated euro stablecoin) automatically via 0xSplits — 90% to creator, 10% to platform Safe. Only verified citizens can participate.

## The Bigger Vision

The same infrastructure for transparent public budget management and electronic voting. If we can govern podcast funds with eIDAS identity, we can govern ALL public funds.

*Svima, a ne samo njima.* — For everyone, not just them.
```
